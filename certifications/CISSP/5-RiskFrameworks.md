# Risk Frameworks

## Risk Frameworks

A process to assess, control monitoring and auditing

NIST SP 800-37 RMF
* RMF Risk mgmt framework

NIST RMF Steps
1. prepare: ID roles, strategies, control providers
2. Categorize: ID all the org assets and categorize in terms of risk and value; document
3. Select: Initial security controls necessary to protect the info sys and org
4. Implement: deploy security controls and doc
5. Asses : test effectiveness of the controls and comply w/ governance
6. AuthorizeL require Sr. Mgmt to take accountability for residual risk
7. Monitor: Ongoing evaluations of security effectiveness against org and system relate changes

ISO 31000 
* 8 principle risk mgmt framework and process 
* provides a common approach to all types of risk, not just technical

Principles
1. Integrated in the whole org
2 Structure and Comprehensive approach to risk Mgmt
3. Customize to the org
4. Inclusive - should include humans and culture of the org in risk Mmgt
5. Dynamic: proactive and adjust to changes
6. Best Available information: make assumptions and contraints
7. Human and cultural factors 
8. Continual improvement

COSO: Community of sponsoring organizations
* enterprise risk mgmt framework focused on corporate governance
* used for Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) 404 requirements
* Components (Start w/ Mission vision and core values)
    * Governance and culture
    * Strategy and Obj Setting
    * Performance
    * Review and Revisions 
    * Information, communication and Reporting
(ends with enhanced value)
