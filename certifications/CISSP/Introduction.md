## Best Practices / Proven Tips

1. Understand the purpose of the exam - leadership focused exam; focus on reducing risk. Scenario based, best possible answer that reduces the most risk

2. Learn and Understand vs Memorizing - more about applying security concepts

3. Create a study plan - break down study into chunks; 1hr/day. Include review time

4. Avoid too many resources

5. Use Practice exams properly. Break scenario down

6. Manage time during the Exam

7. Prepare for Exam Day - relax, good night sleep, etc.
