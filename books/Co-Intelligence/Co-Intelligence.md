## Chapter 03: Four rules for Co-Intelligence 

Principle 1: Always invite AI to the table
* help familiarize yourself with it and capabilities
* not a silver bullet
* avoid dependence by not using as a crutch

Principle 2: Be the human in the loop
* AI wants to make you happy and they don't know anything
* You are accountable for what comes out of AI that you publish

Principle 3: Treat AI like a person (but tell it what kind of person it is)
* it is easier if you think of the AI as an alien person
* Tell the system who it is (persona)
* Add more context and constraints

Principle 4: Assume this is the worst AI you will ever use
